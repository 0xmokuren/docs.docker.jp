=======================================
rm
=======================================

.. code-block:: bash

   Usage: rm [options] [SERVICE...]
   
   Options:
   -f, --force   Don't ask to confirm removal
   -v            Remove volumes associated with containers

.. Removes stopped service containers.

停止済みのサービス・コンテナを削除します。
